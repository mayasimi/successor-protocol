⚠️ The Problem

When someone passes away, their crypto wallets become inaccessible. No one has the keys. No one can claim the assets.

Billions of dollars in Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other crypto sit frozen forever. Families are left with nothing. This is a silent crisis in web3.

Existing solutions require trusted third parties, legal fees, or manual intervention — all of which fail.

✓ The Solution

Dead Man's Agent removes trust from the equation. An autonomous, on-chain agent watches your heartbeat.

When you stop responding, it executes your final wishes — sending assets, notifying loved ones, and closing accounts — all verifiable on-chain.

No lawyers. No delays. No lost crypto.
