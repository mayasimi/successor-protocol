How It Works

Four simple steps to secure your digital legacy forever.
1

Create & Connect

Sign up with your email, connect your wallet, and name your trusted next of kin. They receive a confirmation email and must accept the role — ensuring consent on both sides.

📧 Next of kin confirmation → on-chain attestation recorded
2

Write Your Will

Add instructions that your agent will execute when you're gone. Send USDC, notify contacts, cancel subscriptions, publish farewell messages — anything on-chain or off-chain.

📝 Instructions stored permanently on 0G Storage → immutable, always accessible
3

Send Heartbeats

Regularly ping the on-chain heartbeat contract to prove you're alive. Set your grace period (3–30 days). The agent watches silently — only acting when you stop responding.

❤️ Each heartbeat = on-chain transaction with timestamp + proof
4

Autonomous Execution

If you miss your heartbeat for the full grace period, the agent alerts your next of kin. After they verify your passing, the agent executes every instruction — all attested on-chain forever.

📜 Every action recorded → verifiable, auditable, permanent

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What if I forget to send a heartbeat?

Your next of kin receives a verification link. They must confirm your passing before the agent executes — preventing false triggers.

❓ Can I change my will after creating it?

Yes — anytime before the agent is triggered. Updates are re-uploaded to 0G Storage with new attestations.

❓ What chains are supported?

0G Chain, Ethereum, Polygon, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, BNB Chain, Avalanche, Solana — and any EVM chain.

❓ Is this legally binding?

Every action is attested on-chain with timestamps and cryptographic proof — creating an immutable, verifiable audit trail.
